Ben's Best Horror Movies!

These are the best three horror movies of all time, objectively speaking.

Scream

A slasher movie that manages to deliver the best aspects of the genre, while simultaneously providing meta commentary on classic horror tropes. Wes Craven created an absolute classic with this one.

Hereditary

A movie that reinvented horror for the modern movie landscape, pushing elevated horror into mainstream discussions film discussions.

Saw

A genre-bending pyschological thriller that spawned an entire subgenre. This is a movie with so much more to offer than it's reputation as a bloody, substanceless piece of torture porn.
